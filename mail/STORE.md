A focused Gmail client built for triage, reading, and sending without the clutter. It reads your inbox, generates a plain-language briefing, and sorts messages into action buckets so you spend less time deciding what to deal with.

**Highlights**

- AI inbox briefing that summarizes what's waiting and updates on demand, with a one-tap regenerate
- Smart triage that classifies mail into buckets like Reply Needed, Follow Up, Waiting On, Read Later, and Label & Archive
- Human-readable triage rules you can create, edit, archive, and re-run, with a cache so reclassifying picks up your latest preferences
- Quick actions on every message: archive, snooze with custom dates, trash, mark as spam, reply, and unsubscribe-and-archive in one move
- Keyboard shortcuts and a command palette for compose, search, and refresh so you can move through the inbox fast
- AI-assisted composer and search that turns natural language into Gmail queries, plus draft saving and contact lookup
- Connects securely to your Gmail account, and exposes read and triage context to other approved Moldable apps in your workspace

Install Mail to turn a noisy inbox into a short, sorted list you can clear in minutes.
