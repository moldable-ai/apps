A fast inbox built for triage. Mail is a snappy Gmail client for reading, searching, and sending — with an AI briefing that surfaces what actually needs your attention.

**Highlights**

- Connects to Gmail via OAuth
- Built for speed: triage, search, archive, snooze, and label
- Read messages with attachments and one-click unsubscribe
- Compose and reply inline
- AI briefing summarizes your important unread mail

> Sign in with Google the first time you open it. Credentials are stored locally and never exposed to generated code.
