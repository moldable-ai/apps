Type a curiosity and Microscope turns it into a science image, a depth-shaded 2D relief, and a 3D model you can spin, light, and inspect. It's a hands-on way to explore the natural world, from a single cell to a galaxy, across eleven curated "worlds."

**Highlights**

- Generate educational science images from any prompt with GPT Image 2 (Normal or High quality), with the category and metadata inferred for you.
- Inspect every exploration in 2D or interactive 3D, with auto-rotate and a lighting panel for brightness, warmth, light direction, and height plus Bright/Soft/Cool presets.
- Browse eleven worlds, from Cells & Microbes and the Human Body to Chemistry, Earth & Weather, and Space & Deep Time, each seeded with prompt ideas.
- Backgrounds are removed automatically and a 3D model renders in the background, so the 2D image is usable while the model finishes.
- Choose your 3D engine per render, fal Hunyuan3D for fast textured meshes or Tripo P1 for cleaner low-poly topology, and switch between ready variants in the viewer.
- Regenerate an image, just the 3D model, or retry background removal in place, and move, search, or ask Moldable to explain any specimen.

Connects to fal Hunyuan3D and Tripo P1 for real image-to-3D rendering.

Install it to turn passing curiosity into something you can actually turn over in your hands.
