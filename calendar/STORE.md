Your schedule, beautifully simple. Calendar connects to your Google Calendar and gives you a clean month view with a daily agenda alongside it — so you always know what's next.

**Highlights**

- Connects securely to Google Calendar via OAuth
- Month grid with events on every day, plus a focused daily agenda
- See times, locations, attendees, and meeting links at a glance
- Jump between months effortlessly

> Sign in with Google the first time you open it. Your tokens are stored locally and never leave your machine.
