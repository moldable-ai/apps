Calendar connects securely to your Google Calendar and pairs a full month grid with a focused daily agenda, so you always see both the big picture and what's next. Sign in once and your whole schedule lives in a fast, local-first view.

**Highlights**

- Month grid showing every day's events side by side with a timeline-style agenda for the selected day
- Color-coded events that mirror Google Calendar's color labels, with all-day events pinned to the top
- Click any event for a popover with full time range, location, and a direct "View in Google Calendar" link
- Navigate months by buttons, mouse wheel, or touch swipe, with a one-tap Today jump
- Past events automatically dim and strike through so the day's remaining schedule stands out

**Connects to:** Google Calendar via OAuth. Your tokens and calendar data are stored locally on your device and never leave your machine.

Beyond its own views, Calendar can share your schedule with other Moldable apps you approve, exposing today's events, upcoming events, date-range and keyword search, and RSVP responses so your other tools can act on your real availability. Install it for a private, polished calendar that doubles as schedule context for everything else you build.
