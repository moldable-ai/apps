Remotion turns video into something you can talk to. Create a project, describe the change you want, and watch your composition update in a live preview, then export the finished video to a file.

**Highlights**

- Manage multiple video projects in a sidebar, each with its own resolution, frame rate, and duration, with create, rename, and guarded delete (type-to-confirm).
- Edit videos in plain language: the app feeds your project's context to Moldable's AI chat, which writes and updates the underlying Remotion `Composition.tsx` for you.
- Live preview powered by the Remotion Player, with compositions compiled in-browser so changes show up immediately, no manual build step.
- Render and export your composition to a video file, with progress, cancel, and a completion dialog that lets you copy the path or reveal it in Finder.
- Self-healing edits: composition errors are caught and sent straight to chat as a fix-this prompt, so a broken render becomes the next instruction.
- Configure project settings (name, width, height, fps) any time, with auto-duration handled by the composition itself.

Runs entirely on your machine through Moldable, with no external API keys or services required. Worth installing if you want to produce programmatic, code-backed videos without touching a terminal.
