Reimagine your home, inside and out. Redecorate is a folder-based playground for AI room, exterior, backyard, and floorplan redesigns — drop a photo of any space, tap a style, and watch it transform while the room keeps its bones. Built on GPT Image 2.

**Highlights**

- Organize ideas into folders by room or zone — Living Room, Kitchen, Backyard, Front Exterior, Floorplans — with starter folders ready on day one
- 140+ curated style presets across interior aesthetics, architecture, landscapes, color palettes, room makeovers, and seasonal looks — no design experience needed
- 16 fill-in-the-blank quick edits for targeted changes: swap the flooring, repaint a wall, redo the landscaping, add a feature, change the time of day
- Restyle from your original photo so geometry, windows, and architecture stay intact — or describe a space and generate one from scratch
- Branch freely: every redesign and remix is kept as a render you can return to, with a hold-to-compare against the original
- Favorite the keepers, move designs between folders, change aspect ratio, download, and archive or delete the rest

**Connects to** OpenAI for GPT Image 2 generation and edits, with optional remove.bg for background removal.

Worth installing if you want to actually see a change before you make it — a calm, image-forward gallery where every room idea has a home.
