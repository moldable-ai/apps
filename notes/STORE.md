A fast, local-first notes app for capturing thoughts in markdown and keeping the ones that matter at the top. Write, label, pin, archive, and search across everything from one clean masonry board.

**Highlights**

- Write notes in full markdown with a live editor; cards render a formatted preview right on the board
- Pin important notes so they stay grouped at the top, separate from everything else
- Organize with labels that auto-suggest as you type and become filters in the sidebar
- Search every note by title, content, or label at once, with results grouped by label
- Archive notes to get them out of the way, or send them to Trash with restore and empty-trash controls
- Built on a responsive masonry grid that adapts as your collection grows

Everything stays on your machine in your active workspace, with no external accounts or services to connect. Install it for a quick, organized place to think.
