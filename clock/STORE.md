Clock brings alarms, multiple countdown timers, a stopwatch, and a set of world clocks together in one fast, local-first app. Everything lives in your workspace on your machine, with no accounts to connect, and four tabs across the top switch between each tool.

**Highlights**

- World Clock anchors a large local time and date, then lists any cities you add with their current time plus a relative-day label (Today, Tomorrow, Yesterday, or the weekday) and the hour difference from your zone, for example +3h
- Add cities from a searchable dialog covering common zones and every IANA time zone, each showing its UTC offset before you pick it
- Set alarms with a time picker, label, and per-day repeat toggles that read back as Once, Weekdays, Weekends, or Every day, each with its own on/off switch
- When an alarm fires it surfaces a dismissible banner with a synthesized chime, and one-shot alarms turn themselves off after ringing, just like a phone
- Run several countdown timers at once from 1, 3, 5, 10, 15, and 25-minute presets or a custom hours/minutes/seconds dialog, each with a circular progress ring and play, pause, and reset controls
- Time things with a stopwatch that shows tenths, records laps, and highlights your fastest and slowest splits
- Drive every view from the command palette to jump tabs or add a city, alarm, or timer without leaving the keyboard

Beyond its own tabs, Clock can share timers, alarms, and world clocks with other Moldable apps you approve, so a workflow can check the next alarm or start a timer for you. Install it for a clean, private place to keep time.
