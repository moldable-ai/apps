# Campaign Brief — style guide

Bold, loud-on-purpose creative campaign brief for a marketing/creative team
pitching an integrated campaign to a brand or internal stakeholders. The bundled
sample is a complete 18-slide brief for a fictional brand (Aera) — tailor the
brand, audience, idea, channels, and numbers.

- Palette: warm cream `#faf7f2` (page), ink black `#0a0a0a` (type), hot magenta
  `#db2777` (the ONE accent — eyebrows, ideas, charts, section fields). No
  gradients, no second accent; restraint is what keeps "loud" from going garish.
- Type: display **Clash Display** (Fontshare), body **Inter** (Google). Lean on
  Clash at huge sizes — the deck's energy is in the type scale, not decoration.
- Imagery: `campaign-brief-cover.jpg` (magenta key-visual — cover, close, and the
  creative-territory split) and `campaign-brief-mockup.jpg` (OOH wild-posting /
  billboard mockup — the full-bleed "sample executions" slide).

## Signature decoration

- `.idea` — the giant declarative idea statement (`em` turns a phrase magenta).
  Pair with `.idea-mark` (a small magenta label) for "The big idea" / "The insight".
- `.ii` / `.ii-cell(.solid)` / `.ii-arrow` — the insight→idea flow: two big claim
  plates joined by a magenta arrow; the right plate is solid magenta.
- `.chan` / `.ch` — channel-mix cards with an accent corner tab, a role label
  (Lead/Amplify/Convert/Own), and a budget-weight footer.
- `.kpi-chip` / `.kpi-ring` — KPI target chips: a pill metric with a conic
  progress ring (`--p` = percent to target).
- `.brief-div` / `.brief-div-title` / `.brief-div-ghost` — section dividers on a
  full magenta field with an oversized translucent ghost numeral.
- `.persona` — dark audience-snapshot card with `.persona-pill` trait pills.
- `.callout` / `.callout-k` — magenta-rule callout for the one-line brief.

## Shared components used

`.full-bleed` (cover/executions/close), `.split.reverse` (creative territory),
`.stats` (audience size), `.checks` (why it works), `.steps` (content & assets),
`.timeline` (media flighting), `.bars` + `.kpi-chip` (KPIs), `.donut` + `.table`
(budget), `.quote` (belief), `.runner` footer on content slides.

## Arc

cover → the brief in one line → the audience (persona + stats) → §The idea →
the big idea (giant statement) → why it works (checks) → insight→idea (flow) →
creative territory (split) → §The plan → channel mix (cards) → content & assets
(steps) → media flighting (timeline) → §Measure → KPIs & targets (chips + bars)
→ budget (donut + table) → sample executions (full-bleed mockup) → belief
(quote) → the ask/close.

## Density notes

Keep the idea slides nearly empty — one huge line, lots of cream. Reserve dense
layouts for channels, KPIs, and budget. Numbers stay tabular; magenta is for
emphasis only, never large fills outside the section dividers and one donut/ring.
