# Clean Minimal — style guide

The default "Swiss precision" anchor: restraint is the brand. The bundled sample
is a complete 17-slide quarterly company strategy review — tailor the bets,
owners, and numbers. Keep it quiet; the white space and one blue do the work.

- Palette: near-white `#fcfcfd`, near-black ink `#0b0b0d`, muted grey `#6b7280`,
  and ONE decisive blue `#2f6df6` (primary and eyebrow). No second accent, no
  fills, no gradients. Type: display + body both **General Sans** (Fontshare),
  weight 600 for headings, 400 for copy.
- Imagery: `clean-minimal-cover.jpg` (calm off-white architectural full-bleed on
  the cover) and `clean-minimal-fig.jpg` (quiet paper-and-ruler still life in the
  lead-bet split). Both lean into negative space and a faint cool-blue accent.
- Bespoke: `.mark/.mark-num/.mark-title/.mark-rule` hairline section markers;
  `.idx/.idx-row/.idx-n/.idx-t/.idx-d` refined numbered agenda on hairlines;
  `.bet/.bet-n/.bet-t/.bet-d/.bet-foot` flat strategic-bet cards; `.aside/.aside-k/
.aside-t` a single hairline-left callout (no fill); `.plot` bar-chart baseline;
  `.score` (`.up`/`.flat`/`.down`) two-tone status dots for tables.
- Shared used: `.full-bleed`, `.stats`, `.table` (`.pos`/`.neg`/`.row-em`),
  `.checks`, `.bullets`, `.cards`/`.cols-3`, `.bars`, `.timeline`, `.split`,
  `.quote`, `.steps`, `.runner` footer on every content slide.
- Density notes: mostly left-aligned, headings tight to a short `.kicker.lockup`
  eyebrow with a leading hairline. Tables stay compact; numbers are tabular.
  Never add shadows beyond the subtle card/media defaults. Statement and quote
  slides are the only centered ones — let them breathe.
- Arc: cover → agenda(idx) → baseline(stats+table) → working/not(checks+bullets)
  → §The bets → statement → three bets(cards) → lead-bet deep-dive(split+image)
  → market(bars) → roadmap(timeline) → stop-doing(checks) → resourcing(stats+
  table) → quote → risks(table) → §Decisions → the ask(steps+aside) → close.
