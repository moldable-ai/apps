A calm space to pull up an affirmation that fits your mood, save the ones that land, and come back to them whenever you need a reset.

**Highlights**

- Ten themed collections: Inner Peace, Self Love, Abundance, Courage, Gratitude, Healing, Focus, Connection, Strength, and Joy
- Around 100 hand-written affirmations per category, each shown one at a time on a clean, full-screen viewer
- Tap to shuffle a fresh random affirmation in the category without leaving the screen
- Heart any affirmation to save it; a dedicated Favorites view lets you revisit or remove them anytime
- Favorites sync to your workspace storage so they follow you, with local fallback so nothing gets lost
- Share the current affirmation through your system share sheet or copy it straight to the clipboard

Runs fully local with no accounts or external services to set up. Install it for a quiet, one-tap moment of focus whenever your day needs one.
