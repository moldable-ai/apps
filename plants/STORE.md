A local-first house plant tracker that identifies each plant from a photo, builds an AI care guide, and tells you exactly when to water. Every plant, photo, and watering log lives in your workspace on your machine, with no account to create.

**Highlights**

- Add a plant by dropping, pasting, or uploading a photo; it's identified by vision and gets an auto-generated care profile, or enter the name yourself in a Manual tab
- Watering centerpiece with a cycle-progress ring and plain-language status ("Water today", "Thirsty — 2 days overdue"), a big animated Water button, and an adjustable "every N days" interval stepper
- "Soil still moist?" skip that pushes the next reminder without logging a watering, plus a stretched winter rhythm during seasonal dormancy so you never overwater
- AI care guide laid out as glanceable tiles for light, temperature, humidity, soil, feeding, dormancy, and a toxic / pet-safe badge, with common problems and a one-tap Regenerate
- Home view groups plants into folders by room plus All, Favorites, and Unplaced, with a watering alert bar that can water every due plant at once
- Growth journal strip of dated photos per plant; tap any one to make it the portrait, and search across name, scientific name, nickname, room, and location
- Inline-editable names, room, and spot over a full-bleed hero, markdown notes with quiet auto-save, and Cmd+K commands to add a plant, water everything due, or jump to search

Install it to keep every plant on a schedule it actually likes, without handing your collection to the cloud.
