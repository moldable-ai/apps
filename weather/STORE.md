A keyless, local-first weather app that resolves your location automatically and shows current conditions plus a 7-day forecast at a glance. It runs entirely on your machine with no API key and no account, pulling data straight from Open-Meteo.

**Highlights**

- Current conditions card with temperature, a feels-like reading, humidity, and wind speed, alongside a day- and night-aware condition emoji and plain-language label
- Horizontally scrolling hourly strip covering the next 12 hours, each chip showing the hour, conditions, and temperature
- 7-day forecast rows with high and low, precipitation probability, and a condition icon for each day
- Search for a city through Open-Meteo geocoding, save it as your custom location, or tap "Use IP" to fall back to automatic geolocation
- Automatic IP-based location resolution with layered fallbacks, so the forecast loads without you typing anything
- Fahrenheit or Celsius follows your desktop Today unit setting, with a one-tap refresh and deep-link support for opening pinned coordinates
- A smooth reveal cascade that replays when you switch cities or flip units, and respects your reduced-motion preference

Beyond its own views, Weather exposes current conditions and the forecast over the Moldable App API, so chat and other apps you approve can read the weather for your location. Install it for a fast, private forecast that needs no setup.
