Generate images from a prompt and keep iterating in place, with every edit and variation kept as a branching history you can return to. Built on GPT Image 2, it turns one-off generation into a real creative workspace.

**Highlights**

- Generate from a text prompt, then refine with targeted edits that build on the previous image instead of starting over
- Branch in two directions: spin off a distinct new image from a reference, or edit one as a child, all tracked in the same thread's iteration tree
- Pick aspect ratio (square, portrait, story, landscape, widescreen) and quality (low to high or auto), and regenerate any image at a new ratio
- One-click background removal that saves a transparent PNG as a fresh iteration
- Drag and drop your own images in, zoom to inspect, and copy or download any result
- Manage history freely: delete single iterations or whole threads, retry failed generations, and cancel stuck work

**Connects to** OpenAI for GPT Image 2 generation and edits, with optional remove.bg for background removal.

Worth installing if you want image generation that remembers where you've been, so good directions are never one regenerate away from being lost.
